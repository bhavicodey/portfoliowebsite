I'm on a mission to break barriers in the field of the STEM industry. I got the wonderful opportunity to speak a Ted Talk on the very topic.

I was featured on TIMES of India for my app named the Tasty Toolbox along with questions about my experience being a south asian girl in the STEM industry and how I manage to juggle all of the projects I work on continously.
